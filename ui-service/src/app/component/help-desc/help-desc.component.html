Operating Instructions

 Welcome to COPAAL - A tool for validating facts over Knowledge Graphs
  • To validate a triple, simply enter the subject, object and the property in the respective fields (currently limited to DBpedia resources)
  • Check the "Verbalize" option if you wish to view the fact explanations in natural language text
  • Do not have a triple in mind? No problem, you can also select from examples to auto-populate the input triple
